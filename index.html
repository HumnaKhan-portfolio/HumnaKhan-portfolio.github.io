TRUST Framework | Legacy Infrastructure Baseline Validation

Developed TRUST, an independent cybersecurity framework exploring baseline validation and operational trust assessment within legacy critical infrastructure environments.

The project looks at how dormant threats, undocumented dependencies, and inherited trust relationships can become normalised inside operational baselines, making modern monitoring less effective than expected.

Presented as an interactive analyst simulation, where participants had to weigh containment decisions against business continuity, safety, and operational consequences.
