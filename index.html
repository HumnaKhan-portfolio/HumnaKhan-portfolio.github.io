Featured Project • UTS Tech Festival 2026

TRUST Framework | Legacy Infrastructure Baseline Validation

Developed TRUST, an independent cybersecurity framework exploring baseline validation and operational trust assessment within legacy critical infrastructure environments.

The project looks at how dormant threats, undocumented dependencies, and inherited trust relationships can become normalised inside operational baselines, which can limit the effectiveness of modern monitoring systems.

Presented as an interactive analyst simulation, where participants evaluated containment decisions and their operational consequences.
